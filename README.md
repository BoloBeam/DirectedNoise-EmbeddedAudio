## Project Overview
Directional Noise-Embedded Audio (DNEA) and Infrasound Technology represent the forefront of acoustic innovation. DNEA enables precise, private, and context-aware sound targeting in complex environments, while infrasound research explores frequencies below human hearing for applications in health, safety, and communication. This repository consolidates:
- Peer-reviewed research and technical treatises
- Market and impact analyses
- Strategic business and commercialization plans
- Ethical, legal, and societal implications (ELSI)
- Public awareness and educational resources

**Significance for Investors & Partners:**
These technologies have transformative potential in:
- Medical therapeutics (e.g., non-invasive neuromodulation, bone conduction for hearing accessibility)
- Security and defense (e.g., targeted alerts, crowd control, surveillance)
- Retail and digital signage (personalized marketing, immersive experiences)
- Smart offices and IoT (private notifications, productivity tools)
- Environmental monitoring and disaster detection
